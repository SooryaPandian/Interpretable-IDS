Attack: Generic
Definition:
Generic attacks represent broad cryptographic or protocol abuse patterns not tied to a single exploit family.

Common Indicators in Network Traffic:
- Repetitive algorithmic traffic signatures
- High-frequency packet exchanges with low semantic diversity
- Anomalies crossing multiple protocol contexts

Potential Impact:
- Disruption, bypass attempts, and broad attack staging

Containment and Response:
- Tighten anomaly thresholds by segment
- Enforce protocol validation and baseline policies
- Investigate adjacent suspicious events for campaign linkage
