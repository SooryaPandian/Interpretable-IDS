Attack: Worms
Definition:
Worm attacks self-propagate across systems without direct user interaction.

Common Indicators in Network Traffic:
- Repeated scanning plus rapid connection spread
- Similar payload patterns across many destinations
- Exponential growth in suspicious flows

Potential Impact:
- Fast internal propagation and broad service disruption
- Secondary payload delivery across the environment

Containment and Response:
- Enforce network segmentation and emergency ACLs
- Quarantine infected segments/hosts quickly
- Patch vulnerable systems and verify remediation coverage
