Attack: Analysis
Definition:
Analysis activity typically represents reconnaissance or probing intended to map services and find weak points.

Common Indicators in Network Traffic:
- Repeated low-volume probes across multiple destinations
- Distinctive scan-like timing patterns
- Connection attempts to uncommon service combinations

Potential Impact:
- Pre-attack intelligence gathering
- Increased risk of follow-on exploitation

Containment and Response:
- Correlate with vulnerability scans and host logs
- Block or throttle probing origins
- Increase monitoring around discovered targets
