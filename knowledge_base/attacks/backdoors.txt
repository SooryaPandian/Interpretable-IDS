Attack: Backdoors
Definition:
Backdoor attacks establish covert persistence channels for unauthorized access.

Common Indicators in Network Traffic:
- Unexpected outbound callbacks to rare destinations
- Periodic beaconing behavior
- Connections to ports/protocols not aligned with baseline

Potential Impact:
- Long-term unauthorized control
- Data theft and lateral movement

Containment and Response:
- Isolate affected hosts immediately
- Rotate credentials and invalidate tokens
- Hunt for persistence mechanisms and C2 indicators
