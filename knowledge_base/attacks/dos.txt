Attack: DoS
Definition:
Denial-of-Service attacks overwhelm compute, network, or application resources.

Common Indicators in Network Traffic:
- Sudden high-volume packet or request bursts
- Resource exhaustion symptoms and latency spikes
- Repeated protocol-level flood signatures

Potential Impact:
- Service outage and degraded availability
- Incident response diversion

Containment and Response:
- Apply traffic scrubbing and filtering
- Enable autoscaling and circuit breakers
- Tune WAF/rate-limit controls and upstream protections
