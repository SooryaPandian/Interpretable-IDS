Attack: Fuzzers
Definition:
Fuzzing attacks send malformed or random inputs to trigger errors, crashes, or abnormal behavior.

Common Indicators in Network Traffic:
- High variation in payload patterns and packet sizes
- Repeated unexpected protocol states
- Spikes in request volume to edge services

Potential Impact:
- Service instability
- Exposure of unhandled exceptions and vulnerabilities

Containment and Response:
- Rate-limit anomalous sources
- Capture payload samples for signature engineering
- Patch input-validation and parser weaknesses
